Featured Recipe: Golden Turmeric Latte

Learn how to make our signature anti-inflammatory turmeric latte using our premium organic turmeric powder and other aromatic spices. This comforting drink is perfect for chilly mornings or as an afternoon pick-me-up.

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup milk of your choice
  • 1 tsp Spice Haven organic turmeric powder
  • ½ tsp Spice Haven cinnamon powder
  • ¼ tsp Spice Haven ginger powder
  • Pinch of black pepper (enhances turmeric absorption)
  • 1 tsp honey or maple syrup (optional)
